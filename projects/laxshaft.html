The Idea

Figure 1. 3D render of lacrosse shaft.

I am developing a lacrosse shaft that will determine the speed of a user's shot. The shaft will be a self-contained system, with sensing, power, and processing all on board the shaft. The objective will be to embed the system to return accurate results while minimizing the weight gain to the shaft.

This project is in an early phase of development that mostly involves research. Once I have a sound prototype I will create a business model canvas and business plan to use for acquiring investors.
